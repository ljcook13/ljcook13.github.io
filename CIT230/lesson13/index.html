Seattle Temple

The Seattle Temple is located in the Seattle, Washington area. There are multiple services that the temple offers like performing baptisms, initiatories, endowments, and sealings. There is a cafeteria in the temple, clothing rental is available and there is also a distribution center that is close by to the temple. This temple's main purpose is to help others come closer to God.
